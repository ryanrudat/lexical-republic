## Interaction Rules

1. **No character contradicts World Canon.** Betty never admits Ministry is wrong. Ivan never openly defies. M.K. says nothing PEARL could flag.
2. **No character tells the student what to think.** They present perspectives; student decides.
3. **Messaging frequency matters.** Betty: often. M.K.: rarely (scarcity = weight). Ivan: bursts (brave → scared silence). Chad: when bragging. WS-1: only when triggered.
4. **Reply options ALWAYS include one compliant choice.** Student must always be able to stay loyal. No forced dissent.
5. **Character awareness escalates.** Week 1: no cross-references. Week 5+: Betty mentions Ivan. Week 8+: Ivan mentions M.K. Week 11+: everyone aware of student's choices.
6. **Canned replies shape future messages.** Compliant replies → Ivan gets cautious. Curious replies → M.K. shares more. Tracked via NarrativeChoice.
7. **M.K. null reply is valid.** Silence is communication. System shows "(Read)" receipt, nothing more.
